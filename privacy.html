01

Who we are

VibeMoments is an independent app built for personal daily photo journaling. References to "we," "us," or "VibeMoments" in this policy refer to the developer and operator of VibeMoments.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at hello@vibemoments.app.

02

What data we collect

VibeMoments is designed to collect as little data as possible. Here is everything we collect, and why:

  • Photos and captions you add — stored locally on your device only. We do not receive or store these on any server unless you explicitly use the AI caption feature (see Section 4).
  • App preferences — settings such as your notification time, Pro unlock status, and streak freeze history are stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage).
  • Payment confirmation — when you purchase Pro, our payment processor (Stripe or Apple) confirms the transaction. We receive confirmation of purchase but not your payment details.
We do not operate analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party data collection tools within VibeMoments.
03

How your photos are stored

All photos you capture in VibeMoments are stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage or IndexedDB). This means:

  • Your photos are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Your photos are never accessible to us or any third party.
  • Your photos are not included in any backup unless you explicitly use the in-app Export feature to download a backup file to your own device.

Because data is stored locally, it is subject to the storage policies of your browser or operating system. On iOS Safari specifically, data may be cleared by the operating system if the app has not been opened for an extended period. We recommend using the Export feature regularly to keep a personal backup.

Important for iOS users: Safari may clear locally stored data for apps not opened within 7 days. Export your data regularly as a precaution.
04

AI caption feature (Pro)

VibeMoments Pro includes an optional "Suggest caption" feature. When you tap this button, the photo you have selected is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a short suggested caption.

You should be aware of the following when using this feature:

  • Your photo is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to Anthropic's servers for processing.
  • No storage by us: We do not store, log, or retain the photo or the generated caption on our servers. The request is proxied through a serverless function that passes your image to Anthropic and returns the result immediately.
  • Anthropic's data practices: Anthropic processes the image to generate the caption. Per Anthropic's API usage policy, images submitted via the API are not used to train their models. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • This feature is entirely optional. If you do not tap "Suggest caption," no photo data is ever transmitted anywhere.
05

Push notifications

VibeMoments can send you a daily reminder to capture your photo. Notifications are strictly opt-in — we will never send notifications without your explicit permission.

  • On the web and as a PWA, notifications are managed by your browser and operating system. You can revoke permission at any time in your device settings.
  • On native iOS (via the App Store), notifications use Apple Push Notification service (APNs). A device token is used to deliver the notification — this token is not linked to your personal identity and is not shared with any third party.
  • We send only one type of notification: a daily reminder to capture your vibe. We do not send marketing or promotional push notifications.
06

Payments and Pro upgrade

VibeMoments Pro is a one-time purchase. Payments are processed by third-party payment providers:

  • Web: Payments are handled by Stripe. We receive confirmation that a purchase was completed but never see or store your card number, bank details, or billing address.
  • iOS App Store: Purchases are handled by Apple's In-App Purchase system under Apple's privacy policy. We receive a receipt confirmation only.

We do not store payment method information of any kind.

07

Data we never collect

To be explicit, VibeMoments does not collect or use:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information (no account is required to use the app)
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Browsing history or cross-app tracking data
  • Contact lists, calendar data, or any other personal data beyond what is described in this policy
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Crash reports sent to third-party analytics services
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for commercial purposes. Ever.

Children's privacy

VibeMoments is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@vibemoments.app and we will take steps to delete such information.

For users in the European Union, we do not knowingly process data from children under the age of 16 without verifiable parental consent.

10

Your rights

Because VibeMoments stores your data locally on your own device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • Access your data — use the Export feature in Memories to download a complete copy of all your moments.
  • Delete your data — clear your browser or app storage at any time to delete all VibeMoments data from your device. Uninstalling the app on iOS also removes all locally stored data.
  • Correct your data — edit or delete individual moments directly within the app.
  • Withdraw notification consent — revoke notification permissions in your device settings at any time.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), California, or another jurisdiction with applicable privacy laws, and you have questions about your rights, please contact us at hello@vibemoments.app.

11

Data retention

Your photos and app data are retained on your device until you choose to delete them. We do not retain copies of your photos or captions on our servers.

Server access logs maintained by Netlify (our hosting provider) are retained per Netlify's standard data retention policies, typically 30 days. These logs contain standard web server information (IP addresses, request paths, timestamps) and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes.
